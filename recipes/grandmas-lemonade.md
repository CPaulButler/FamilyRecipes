# Grandma's Fresh Lemonade

**Type:** Drink  
**Flavor:** Sweet  
**From:** Linda

## Description
A refreshing homemade lemonade recipe perfect for summer gatherings.

## Ingredients
- 6 fresh lemons
- 1 cup sugar
- 6 cups cold water
- Ice cubes
- Fresh mint leaves (optional)

## Instructions
1. Roll lemons on counter to soften them
2. Cut lemons in half and squeeze juice into a pitcher (should yield about 1 cup of juice)
3. Add sugar to the lemon juice and stir until dissolved
4. Add cold water and stir well
5. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving
6. Serve over ice with fresh mint leaves if desired

## Notes
Grandma always made this in a big glass pitcher for Sunday family dinners. She said the secret was using fresh lemons and not being shy with the sugar!
